Orderly ("小物记") is a mobile application developed by an individual developer (referred to as "we," "us," or "the developer") designed to help users record and manage personal items. This privacy policy is intended to inform you about how we collect, use, and protect your information. Please read this privacy policy carefully before using the app.

Contact Information: gentleming1986@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We respect your privacy and are committed to minimizing data collection.

2.1 Personal Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email address, phone number, etc.).

2.2 Non-Personal Information

For the purpose of diagnosing errors, optimizing app performance, and analyzing usage, we may collect the following non-personal information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version.
  • App Usage Information: Anonymized app usage logs to understand how features are used so we can improve the app.

2.3 Location Information

We do not collect your precise or approximate location information.

2.4 Sensitive Data

We do not collect any sensitive data, such as photos, contacts, calendar data, or health data. Any photos and text you record within the app are stored exclusively on your device.

3. How We Use the Information We Collect

The non-personal information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Error Diagnosis and Performance Optimization: To help us identify and fix bugs and crashes within the app.
  • App Analytics: To understand how users interact with the app so we can improve its features and user experience.

We do not use your information for personalized advertising or any other marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data Storage: All user data, including item information you record in the app, is stored locally on your device only. This data is encrypted to ensure security.
  • Data Deletion: When you uninstall the app, all data stored on your device will be automatically deleted.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any of your information with any third parties.

We do use third-party SDKs to support app functionality. These SDKs may collect non-personal information in accordance with their respective privacy policies. The main third-party services we use include:

  • Analytics Services: To collect anonymous app usage data to help us understand and improve the app.
  • Apple Payment: To process in-app purchases (IAP). All payment information is handled by Apple, and we do not have access to your payment details.

6. Children's Privacy Policy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app. This app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR-K).

7. Your Rights

As a user of Orderly, you have the following rights:

  • Access, Modify, and Delete Data: You can view, edit, or delete all the data you have recorded at any time within the app.
  • Unsubscribe: You can manage and cancel any in-app subscriptions through the App Store.
  • Permission Management: You can manage permissions for the app, such as notifications and camera access, in your device's "Settings."

8. Payment Processing

This app supports in-app purchases (IAP). All payment transactions are securely processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect or process any of your payment information.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be notified to you through in-app prompts. You can always view the latest version of the privacy policy within the app.
